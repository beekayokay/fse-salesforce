We are pleased to respond to your inquiry for replacement control panel for your FS-Elliott Air Compressor. The R1000 focuses on energy efficiency and enhanced user accessibility. This easy-to-use, multifaceted control system empowers plant operators to master one of the facility’s highest operating expenses – energy costs.  
I. EASE OF INSTALLATION

  • The R1000 control panel arrives to the consumer ready for retrofit to the plant’s compressor.
  • Since each control panel is completely assembled before shipment, all that is required for operation are the plant’s instrumentation, communication, and power connections.
  • The panel housing is made of carbon-steel, or optional stainless steel, and has proper sealing to protect all circuitry elements from damage.
  • All of the control and instrument components within the confines of the package are wired and/or piped.
 
II. EASE OF MAINTENANCE
  • Every control panel incorporates a fail-safe design in case of an operation malfunction.
  • The custom operating system is designed to meet the consumer’s requirements and stored onto a memory card, which allows easy replacement/installation during maintenance to the control panel’s programming code.
  • The control panel operates independently from the human machine interface (HMI) screen. This ensures that the control panel will continue to operate normally if the HMI is not working correctly.
  • The R1000 software is designed for simple maintenance checks and software updates using a standard USB Port.
